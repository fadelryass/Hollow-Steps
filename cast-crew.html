Who we are ?

We are a passionate team of students from the SAE Institute Leipzig, coming together from different departments to collaborate on an exciting creative media project. With additional support from talented external contributors, we are pushing our creative boundaries to bring a thrilling horror short film to life as part of our module project.

Our goal is to craft an immersive and chilling cinematic experience, blending storytelling, sound design, visual effects, and cinematography. This project is a true testament to teamwork, dedication, and the power of interdisciplinary collaboration. Stay tuned as we bring our vision to the screen!
